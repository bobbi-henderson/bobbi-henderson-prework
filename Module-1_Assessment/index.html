About Me

Hello, my name is Bobbi, and I am single and ready to mingle!... Just kidding, this isn't that kind of about me section. I am a young professional who is looking to broaden my horizons and learn new skills. I have always had a passion for learning, and ever since graduating from the University of Minnesota in 2015, I have been seeking out self-lead learning opportunities. Currently, I am focusing my efforts on learning the art of coding. I have been learning to code in my free time over the last eight months or so, and I have been really enjoying the journey. While most of my professional experience has been in Customer Service, I have found that learning to code has allowed me to apply the problem-solving skills I have gained over my career in a more analytical way. I am very excited about the possibility to change careers into software development, and I welcome the constant learning that comes with entering the field.

Aside from my passion of learning new things, some of my other hobbies include reading, watching movies, going to concerts, traveling, and listening to podcasts. Due to the current COVID-19 pandemic I am definitely missing some of my favorite hobbies, like frequenting movie theaters, concerts, and traveling as much as I can. I have been trying to focus that time into the things I still can do. I have added multiple new podcasts into my regular rotation, and I have been able to read a lot more as well. I just finished reading "All Systems Red" the first book in The Murderbot Diaries by Martha Wells. It is honestly one of the best books I have read in recent memory, and I am eager to continue with the rest of the series. I would absolutely recommend it to anyone who loves Science Fiction, or to someone who is looking for a good place to start in the genre.

I also want to give a quick shoutout to my Minnesotan roots! Even though I spent some time living in the great state of Florida, I found my way back to Minnesota after only two years because Minnesota is awesome! I recommend moving to Minnesota for anyone who is looking to relocate. Don't let the snow scare you off, we have some of the nicest people you will ever meet, and the summers are second to none! Lastly, Go Wild!
